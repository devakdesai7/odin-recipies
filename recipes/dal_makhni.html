Dal Makhni


Description

Dal Makhni is an authentic Indian dish made with cereals and various spices in it for the masaledar taste of India, it is served as a part of the punjabi, gujarati and marwadi cuisines but having its roots in Punjab. We'll see its recipe ahead in this article that you can make with chapatis or rice.

Recipe

  1. Prepare the ingredients
  2. Switch on the gas and put up the cereals in the presusre cooker and the cooker on the gas
  3. Once the dal is boiled and soft in the cooker (mostly after 2 cooker calls), take the dal out in a container
  4. Put an aluminium container on the gas and add oil to it
  5. Once the oil boils, add a pinch of asaphoetida in it
  6. Now add other spices
    • Red mirch powder
    • Salt
    • A hint of Tamarind
    • A pinch of black pepper
    • Dal Makhni masala of your choice
  7. Now add the boiled dal to the container on the stove in which the spices are and add some water to it so that the dal seems as a smooth paste when stirred
  8. And HURRAH! Your Dal makhni is ready
