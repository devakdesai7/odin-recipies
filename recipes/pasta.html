Pasta

  • Red Sauce Pasta

    Recipe

    1. Prepare the ingredients
    2. Add enough water with a hint of oil to an aluminium container and add the required amount of pasta to it and switch on the gas
    3. Now wait for the pasta to boil which you will know when the water will boil
    4. Once the pasta is boiled, remove the water out and take pasta in a different container
    5. Take 3-4 tomatoes and add them to a grinder's big container and grind them together with half a cup of water and some cloves and basil leaves along with 2 kashmiri red chilles, this is the red sauce
    6. Now, put another container in which you want to make the pasta on the stove and add some oil and butter in it
    7. NOw add the red sauce to the container on the stove
    8. stir the sauce and after a while, add the veggies you would like to have such as
      • Bell peppers of different colours
      • Brocolli
      • Corn
      • Zucchuni
    9. ONce everything gets mixed, add a pinch of sugar to the final mixture to balance the sourness of tomatoes
    10. Add the boiled pasta to the final mixture along with pizza-pasta sauce and chilli flakes and orgeano and stir well
    11. Add some cheese at the end if you like
